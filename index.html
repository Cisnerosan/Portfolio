About Me

My name is Alba N. Cisneros I'm a El Paso, TX native, received my BA in Criminal Jusice with a Minor in Forensic Psychology from NMSU. I've been a volunteer at sexual assault crisis center, I've also been a substitute teacher for EPISD (El Paso Independent School Disctric); I've been employed with Doña Ana County as a correctional officer; worked with youth as a youth care worker which allowed me to work with youth between the ages of 7-17 yoa's, in this field I worked closely with youth that came from the southern countries seeking assistance in the U.S. and this job allowed us to assist them staying in the U.S. legally.

In 2011 I began working at the El Paso Communications Department as a Public Safety Communicator, was cross trained to handle both incoming emergency calls as well as dispatch units to emergecies. I am currently working for a non-profit agency in which I work with At-Risk youth with the sole purpose to keep the students in school.
